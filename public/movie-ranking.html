Prerna's Movie Recommendations

My TOP 3 Movies of all-time.

Your Name

Beautiful aesthetics, great story, storytelling and direction. I loved the mixing of several different concepts.

Andhadhun

A great suspense thriller with constant plot twists. Amazing storyline and great work by the actors.

Brahmastra

Fantastic graphics. Best enjoyed when watched in 3D. Lacks in a strong storyline but its worth watching if watching in theatres in 3D. Amazing romantic thriller. I thoroughly enjoyed the visuals.
